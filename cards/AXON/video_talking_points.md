# AXON — Video Talking Points

- Axon makes body cameras and tasers but is betting everything on becoming a software platform for police departments.
- Government digitization is accelerating and once departments integrate these systems the switching costs become massive.
- The bull case is they become the operating system for law enforcement with AI analytics and premium features.
- The risk is paying 2,000 times free cash flow for a business model transformation that hasn't happened yet.
- It's a watch until August earnings show software revenue growing fast enough to justify this valuation.
