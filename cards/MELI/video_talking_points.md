# MELI — Video Talking Points

- MercadoLibre runs Latin America's largest e-commerce and payments ecosystem across 18 countries.
- They're capturing a multi-decade digital transformation in markets with low e-commerce penetration.
- The company generates $11.8B in free cash flow while trading at only 7x that number.
- Brazil represents 60% of revenue, creating dangerous single-point-of-failure risk.
- Buy rating - the dominant moat and reasonable valuation outweigh the concentration risks.
