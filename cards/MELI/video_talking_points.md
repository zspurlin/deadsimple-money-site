# MELI — Video Talking Points

- MercadoLibre runs Latin America's largest e-commerce and payments ecosystem across 18 countries.
- They're investing $4.6B in Mexico while generating $11.8B in free cash flow annually.
- The company has genuine network effects and is becoming the financial backbone for underbanked Latin Americans.
- Brazil dependency creates single-point-of-failure risk and four straight earnings misses are concerning.
- At 7x free cash flow, the valuation assumes modest expectations despite the dominant moat.
