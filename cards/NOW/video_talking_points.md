# NOW — Video Talking Points

- ServiceNow builds cloud workflow automation software for large enterprises managing IT and business processes.
- AI is transforming how enterprises handle workflows, creating both opportunity and commoditization risk.
- Their decade of workflow data across thousands of companies gives them AI training advantages competitors can't match.
- Main risk is AI making custom workflow creation so easy that enterprises bypass ServiceNow entirely.
- Strong buy based on defensible data moats, but watch for revenue growth below 15% signaling faster AI commoditization.
